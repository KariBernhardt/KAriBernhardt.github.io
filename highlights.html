    DNP Project

  • DNP Comprehensive Paper
    • I am thrilled to have completed my Doctor of Nursing Practice Project, which was an evidence-based practice project focused on fertility awareness. Along with my two academic partners, we implemented a menstrual cycle health education program for the University of Mary St. Gianna School of Health Sciences. This project aimed to improve college students' knowledge and understanding of fertility & reproductive health for personal and professional use. Completing this project was a significant achievement for me, as it allowed me to apply the knowledge and skills I had gained throughout my academic and professional career to develop an innovative approach to nursing education and patient care. I am proud to have contributed to advancing the field of nursing and to have made a positive impact on the lives of patients and students alike.
