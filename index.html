Kari Bernhardt
Doctor of Nursing Practice

As a driven and compassionate nursing professional, I am currently pursuing a Doctorate in Nursing Practice at the University of Mary, building on my Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the same institution in 2018. My nursing experience includes working in emergency & trauma and labor and delivery units, providing compassionate care to patients in critical situations. As a team player, I work collaboratively with colleagues and interdisciplinary teams to deliver patient-centered care that emphasizes the unique needs of individuals.

In addition to my academic and professional achievements, I am also the proud recipient of the Loretta Ford Centennial Scholarship, which recognizes exceptional nursing students who demonstrate outstanding potential in the field. Furthermore, completing a medical mission in the Philippines has significantly impacted my perspective on healthcare, inspiring me to become a more compassionate and culturally competent provider. As I continue to grow in my career, I am committed to delivering patient-centered care that addresses the unique needs of each individual, while upholding the values of the nursing profession. My passion for learning drives me to continually expand my knowledge and skills, and my commitment to providing high-quality, holistic care ensures that my patients receive the best possible outcomes.
